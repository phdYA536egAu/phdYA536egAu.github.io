Rules

Game goals

This is a game for 3 or more players. Each player except one gets a card with a location, which is the same for everyone. One of the players gets a spy card. Nobody knows who is who.

Spy's goal:

To stay undetected, until the time expires, or to guess the location

Other players' goal:

To make the right choice who the spy is.

If one of the players thinks that he/she has detected the spy, participants start voting. If all players who are not spies vote for the right person, the spy comes out. In this case, he/she tries to name the location. If the spy guesses correctly, he/she wins. Otherwise the team wins. If the player is not a spy, the game continues

How to play

1. Chose your settings: number of players, number of spies and dictionary

2. Press play

3. Press 'View Card' to view your card

4. Press 'Hide Card' to hide your card and pass the phone/computer to the person beside you

5. Repeat 3 and 4 until all players have seen their card

6. Set a timer ( Recommended: between 5 and 10 minutes )

Have fun!
